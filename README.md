# Entrata IPO Valuation and Forecasting

## Overview

This project analyzes the proposed IPO of Entrata, a cloud-based property management software platform. The objective was to build a KPI-driven financial model, forecast operating performance, estimate valuation, and provide an investment recommendation.

**Recommendation:** SUBSCRIBE

**Target Price:** $24/share

**IPO Range:** $22–$26/share

---

## Objectives

* Analyze Entrata's business model and market opportunity
* Forecast revenue using key operational drivers
* Estimate future profitability and operating leverage
* Perform scenario and sensitivity analyses
* Determine a fair value using EV/Revenue multiples

---

## Methodology

### Revenue Model

Revenue was forecast using:

**Revenue = Units × ARPU**

Key assumptions:

* Unit Growth: 12% (2026E), 11% (2027E), 10% (2028E)
* ARPU Growth: 7% (2026E), 6% (2027E), 5% (2028E)

### Margin Forecast

* Gross Margin: 63% to 65%
* Operating Margin: 26% to 31%

Margin expansion assumptions reflect SaaS operating leverage and scale benefits.

### Valuation Approach

* Method: EV/Revenue Multiple
* Base Multiple: 8.5× 2026E Revenue
* Valuation Scenarios:

  * Bear Case: 7.8×
  * Base Case: 8.5×
  * Bull Case: 9.2×

---

## Key Findings

* Revenue forecast grows from **$509.3M in 2025A to $830.8M in 2028E**
* Gross retention remains strong at **97%**
* Margin expansion supports improving profitability
* AI-enabled products and cross-selling drive ARPU growth
